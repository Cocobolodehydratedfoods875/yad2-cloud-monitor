## 🖥️ System requirements

This tool runs on most modern versions of Windows. You do not need powerful hardware to track your listings.

- Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
- Memory: 4GB of RAM or more.
- Storage: 100MB of free disk space.
- Internet: A stable connection to reach the Yad2 servers.

## 🛠️ Setting up the software

After the download finishes, locate the file in your Downloads folder. Double-click the file to start the installer. Windows might show a security prompt because the app comes from a new source. If you see a blue box, click "More info" and then "Run anyway." Follow the on-screen instructions to finish the installation process.

Once the installation finishes, you will see a shortcut icon on your desktop. Double-click this icon to launch the application.

## 🔑 Connecting your account

The first time you open the program, it asks for your login details. Enter the email and password you use for your Yad2 account. This information stays on your device. The program uses these details only to fetch the status of your specific listings. It does not send your password to third-party servers.

## 📊 Viewing your listings

The main window shows a list of all your active ads. Each entry includes:

- Ad Title: The name of the item you want to sell.
- Views: How many users clicked on your ad.
- Last Update: The date when you last modified the listing.
- Status: Labels such as "Active," "Pending," or "Expired."

If you have many ads, use the search bar at the top to filter items by title. Click any row to see more details about a specific listing.

## 🔔 Enabling notifications

The monitor can alert you when your ads need attention. Go to the Settings tab to toggle these alerts. You can choose to get a desktop notification if an ad expires or if you receive a new message through the Yad2 platform. 

## 🔄 Refreshing your data

The program updates your listings automatically every hour. If you want to see the latest data right away, click the Refresh button in the top menu. This action reaches out to the Yad2 servers and pulls the most recent information. A green checkmark at the bottom of the window confirms that the data is current.

## 🛡️ Privacy and security

Your privacy matters. This application acts as a bridge between your computer and the Yad2 website. It only retrieves information that is already visible to you through your browser. No human monitors your activity. The software stores your session token locally so you do not have to sign in every time you open the app. You can clear this saved data anytime by selecting "Sign out" from the menu.

## 💡 Troubleshooting common issues

If you face problems, follow these steps to find a fix:

- Log in again: Sometimes your session expires. Sign out and sign in to refresh your connection.
- Check the time: Ensure your computer clock matches the regional time. A wrong clock can cause issues with server authentication.
- Update the app: Check the website occasionally to see if a newer version exists. Updates often fix minor bugs or improve performance.
- Restart: If the interface stops responding, close the app completely and open it again.
- Network: Confirm that your internet connection is active. The app cannot fetch data if your computer is offline.

## 📝 Frequently asked questions

Does this tool cost money?
No, the tool is free to use.

Can I manage multiple accounts?
This version supports one account at a time. 

Does this tool delete my ads?
No, the tool does not have permission to delete items. It only displays status information.

What happens if I forget my password?
The tool uses your existing Yad2 password. If you forget your password, you must reset it on the official Yad2 website.

Can I leave the app open in the background?
Yes, the app sits in your system tray by the clock when you minimize it. It continues to monitor your ads without taking up space on your taskbar.
